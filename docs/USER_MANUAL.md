## üë®‚Äçüíº For Admins

### 1. Logging In
* Go to `/admin/login.html`.
* Enter your username and password.

### 2. Dashboard Overview
* **KPI Cards:** See how many guests are arriving, leaving, or owe deposits today.
* **Today's List:** A quick list of today's actions.
    * Click **"WA Deposit"** to WhatsApp a guest for their deposit.
    * Click **"WA Check-in"** to send check-in instructions.
* **Quick Add:** Use the form at the top to quickly record a booking (e.g., from a phone call).

### 3. Managing Bookings
* Navigate to **Bookings** in the menu.
* **Search:** Type a name or phone number to find a reservation.
* **Edit:** Click "Edit" to change dates, update payment status, or add notes.
* **Cancel:** Open "Edit" and click "Cancel Booking".

### 4. Cleaning Management
* Navigate to **Cleaning**.
* You will see a list of check-outs for the next 21 days.
* The system automatically lists the "Cleaner" assigned to the previous booking.
* Click **"Open Checklist"** to see specific cleaning requirements for that unit.

## üßπ For Cleaners

### 1. Accessing Your Tasks
* Go to `/cleaner/login.html` on your phone.
* Log in with your cleaner account.

### 2. Viewing Schedule
* You will see a list of units that need cleaning **today**.
* Each card shows:
    * **Unit Name**
    * **Checkout Time** (When the guest leaves)
    * **Notes** from the Admin.

### 3. Completing a Job
* Once you finish cleaning a unit, click the **"Mark Done"** button.
* This updates the Admin immediately so they know the room is ready for the next guest.
